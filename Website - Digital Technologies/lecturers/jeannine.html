Biography

Jeannine has 20 years of work experience in project management, change management, operations management and communications roles. She has worked in a range of fields, including software development, tourism, airports, venture capital, film postproduction, education, government agencies and non-profits in Germany, Canada and New Zealand.

Jeannine has an undergraduate degree in Airport Management and two Masters Degrees in International Relations and Human Rights.


Research interests

Human Rights, Refugees and migrants, Europe and New Zealand, Youth unemployment, Foreign Affairs und cross-boundary human rights obligations, Humanitarian Intervention.
