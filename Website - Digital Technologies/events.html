IoT Day Mini-conference by IoT Auckland

We are celebrating the occasion with a mini-conference for you to discover the Internet of Things and the individuals leading the way in New Zealand. We are show casing a number of Internet of Things ventures, representing several project teams and organisations. This will be a lively, informative and probably surprising revelation of IoT applications. Join with us to discover amazing people working on IoT systems, how they are creating new opportunities and changing the world we live in. The morning will open with a key note address outlining Internet of Things opportunities and the New Zealand context. Each show case venture will present their product and explain their business objective. Followed by a panel discussion to analyse what we have heard and provide insights on the opportunities and challenges.
